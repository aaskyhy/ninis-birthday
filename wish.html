Dear Ninis,

Happy 23rd birthday, Ninis. 🎂

Thank you for existing and for being part of so many meaningful memories in my life. I’m truly thankful that life allowed us to become friends. Somehow our paths crossed, and we grew into friends who always look out for each other.

Even if life feels heavy sometimes, please remember that you are never alone. There will always be people who care about you deeply, and I will always be one of them.

Even on days when it feels like nothing special has been done, or when you feel like you are not enough, one thing will always be true: just by being yourself, you already mean something important to the people who care about you.

I may not always text or reach out all the time, but please know that my care and appreciation for you will always be there. And if one day you ever need someone to talk to, someone to listen, or someone to simply be there for you, I will always be here.

And one day, the right people will see you properly too. Not because you became someone else, but because you stayed as you.

So please don’t be too hard on yourself. The world may be harsh sometimes, but you deserve softness, peace, and a future filled with beautiful things.

I pray that Allah will always protect you and grant you good health, both physically and mentally. May Allah ease your heart, strengthen your iman, and fill your days with peace and happiness.

May Allah ease every difficulty in your studies, bless all the effort you put into your degree, and guide you towards the career and future that is best for you.

May Allah grant you success in this world and the next, surround you with sincere people who love and appreciate you, and answer every dua that you whisper in your prayers.

Love Always,
Aina Yahya
